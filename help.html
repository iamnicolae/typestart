Help

How to play

1. Type "start" in the text box to start the game.

2. You have 10 seconds to play. Type out each word as fast as you can.

3. For each word you type correctly, you get 1 extra second.

Good luck.

Statistics

To view your past scores and also your highest score go to the Statistics page. Your scores are only stored local in browser.

Language

To try out words in different languages, change the language from the top right dropdown.
